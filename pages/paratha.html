Paratha

Description

Paratha is some pretty good stuff. I have it almost daily. You should try it. Follow the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Oil/ghee
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Take some Flour
  2. Add some salt
  3. Add some water
  4. Mix em togther
  5. Cook it with oil/ghee
